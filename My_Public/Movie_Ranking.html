The Best Movies According to Sri Sakthi

My Top 5 movies of All-Time

Ok Kanmani

This is my most favorite Tamil Movie of all time.The best love genre movie ever by ManiRatnam


Soorarai Pottru

This is a Real-Life Story of a Young man from a Remote Village of India, Who dreams of launching his own Airline Service accessible to Everyone. This Movie shows the Challenges he has to overcome in order to be successful in his quest.


Jai-Bhim

This movie is based on a true-incident in 1993, A brave Activist-lawyer fights for justice when a poor tribal man,who gets falsely accused of robbery, goes missing from the police custody.


Five-Feet Apart

This is a great English movie that shows the life of Cancer Patients in a Hospital and the love between the lead Characters!


First Romance

This is a Chinese Web-series, Which I loved! It's a simple and cool School Love Story. It's a Feel Good Movie!
